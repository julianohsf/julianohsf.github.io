Personality

Assertive Protagonist (ENFJ-A)

A Protagonist is a warm and forthright person that loves helping others, and they tend to have strong ideas and values. They back their perspective with the creative energy to achieve their goals.

Protagonists are natural-born leaders, full of passion and charisma. Forming around two percent of the population, they oftentimes reaching out and inspiring others to achieve and to do good in the world. With a natural confidence that begets influence, they take a great deal of pride and joy in guiding others to work together to improve themselves and their community.
